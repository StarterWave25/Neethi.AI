How Neethi.AI Works ?

1

User sends a message

This can be a news headline, WhatsApp forward, or social media post.

2

Neethi understands the language and meaning

It works in any language, even regional ones like Telugu, Hindi, or Tamil.

3

It searches trusted news sources

Like PIB, The Hindu, NDTV, India Today, and regional news portals.

4

AI checks the facts

Neethi compares the claim with real news and scores its truth.

5

You get a clear answer

It tells you if the news is ✅ Real or ⚠️ Fake, along with the confidence level and source links.
