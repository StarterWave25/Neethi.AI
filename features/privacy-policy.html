Privacy Policy

1. What We Collect

  • The text, link, or image you submit for checking
  • Basic metadata (like date/time of submission)
  • We do not collect personal information (like your name, email, phone)

2. Why We Collect It

We collect this info only to:

  • Check if the content is fake or real
  • Improve the accuracy of our AI agent
  • Maintain a record of previous submissions (in History)

We never sell, share, or use your data for ads.

3. How We Protect Your Data

  • We use secure systems to store and process submissions
  • Your data is anonymized and stored only for analysis or feedback
  • We do not track your location, device, or identity

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted services like:

  • Tavily Search API (for news results)
  • LLMs like Gemini or OpenAI (to analyze claims)

These services only receive the submitted content — not your personal info.

5. Your Rights

  • You can request deletion of any submission from the History section
  • You can use the tool without logging in (we don’t need your personal details)
  • We don’t use cookies for tracking

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about privacy, email us at: support@neethi.ai

Our Promise

We built Neethi.AI to protect truth — and that includes protecting you.

Your privacy is not just a setting — it’s a principle we follow in every line of code.
